Guitar Rental Services and Agreements

  • A $50 security deposit, which will be fully refunded upon return, is required to confirm all reservations.
  • A valid government-issued photo ID, such as a driver's license or passport, is required at the time of rental.
  • All renters must be at least 18 years old, with proof of age, to rent a guitar from our shop.
  • Enjoy unlimited playtime during the entire rental period, allowing for extensive practice and performance.
  • Basic liability coverage, protecting against accidental damage, is automatically included with each rental agreement.
  • Comprehensive damage protection plans, offering enhanced security, are available for purchase at an additional cost.
  • Free delivery of guitars to hotels or Airbnbs is provided for rental periods of two or more consecutive days.
  • Payment methods accepted include cash, major credit cards, and widely accepted debit cards for your convenience.
  • Guitars may be conveniently returned to either one of our two rental locations during our normal business hours.
  • Guitars must be returned in the same excellent condition as they were received, barring normal wear and tear.
