Gibson Dove

The Gibson Dove: A legendary square-shoulder acoustic, born in the heart of the '60s music revolution. Its flame maple back and sides deliver a crisp, powerful tone, balanced by the warmth of its square-shoulder design. Classic Dove appointments include a bone nut and saddle, vintage binding, Grover Keystone tuners, and the iconic Dove pickguard with its hand-engraved mother-of-pearl inlay

Gibson SJ-200

The Gibson SJ-200: "King of the Flat-Tops." Since 1937, this iconic Super Jumbo has been the choice of countless artists. Its striking features include the carved Moustache bridge, engraved tortoise pickguard, and mother-of-pearl crown inlays. With exquisite flamed maple back and sides, the SJ-200 is a true American original.

Martin D50

Celebrate 50 years of Chris Martin IV's leadership with the D-50 CFM IV 50th Anniversary. This limited-edition masterpiece features Guatemalan rosewood and a heavy bearclaw spruce top, accented by European flamed maple binding and intricate abalone/mother-of-pearl inlays. Each of the 50 available guitars bears a signed, numbered label, honoring Chris's dedication to crafting the world's finest acoustics.

Martin Grand J16e

The Martin Grand J-16E 12-String adds a shimmering dimension to your music. Handcrafted in Nazareth, this 16 Series guitar blends the projection of a Grand Jumbo with the comfort of an Auditorium. Its satin spruce top and East Indian rosewood back and sides deliver rich tone, while the Martin E1 electronics with onboard controls and tuner ensure you're always ready to perform.

Taylor 815e

Celebrate 50 years of Taylor with the 815e Legacy Jumbo. This bold acoustic-electric boasts a responsive Sitka spruce top and rosewood back and sides for powerful projection. Ideal for strummers and fingerpickers, its comfortable mahogany neck and ebony fingerboard offer exceptional playability. The LR Baggs Element VTC pickup delivers authentic plugged-in tone. The 815e Legacy marks a triumphant return to Taylor's biggest, most resonant body shape.

Taylor Legacy 855e

Celebrate Taylor's 50th with the 855e Legacy 12-string Jumbo. This powerful acoustic-electric features a Sitka spruce top and rosewood back and sides for exceptional projection and shimmering tone. Perfect for strummers and fingerpickers, its comfortable mahogany neck and ebony fingerboard offer unmatched playability. The LR Baggs Element VTC pickup delivers authentic amplified sound. The 855e Legacy is a triumphant return to Taylor's biggest, boldest 12-string design.
