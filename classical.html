Cordoba Esteso

The Cordoba Esteso SP classical guitar honors renowned luthier Domingo Esteso, blending classic design with modern updates. It features a solid European spruce top and solid pau ferro back and sides, delivering a balanced tone with punchy treble, strong mids, and warm lows. This combination provides full, ringing notes and chords with a quick decay, perfect for classical music's harmonic clarity. A mahogany neck and ebony fingerboard ensure smooth playability for intricate techniques.

Cordoba Friederich

The Cordoba Luthier Select Friederich is a handcrafted nylon-string classical guitar, inspired by luthiers Daniel Friederich and Robert Bouchet. Built in small batches, it features a solid Canadian cedar top and solid rosewood back and sides, with rosewood binding, producing a balanced tone with strong lows, clear mids, and highs. The mahogany neck and ebony fingerboard provide excellent playability. A thin polyurethane finish protects the guitar without impacting its sound.

Ramirez Centenario

The rare 2008 Jose Ramirez Centenario Cedar Top Classical Guitar, in perfect condition with a hard case, celebrates 100 years of Ramirez guitar making. Built by Amalia Ramirez, it uses the workshop's finest, long-collected materials, including a first-pick cedar top and rosewood back/sides. It features significant detailing: a sculpted heel cap, headstock inlays, tapered ebony on the neck's back, and intricate wood inlays. Tuners are Rodgers. While similar in dimensions to other 1a models, this guitar represents the workshop's best work. It continues the Ramirez tradition, known for instruments played by professionals like Sting, Segovia, and Christopher Parkening.

Ramirez Legado

The Ramirez Legado is a custom, handmade Manzano case, emblazoned with the Ramirez logo. This is the top-tier presentation of a Ramirez guitar. This premium case is not just a means of transport; it's a protective sanctuary, designed to safeguard the instrument's beauty and structural integrity for generations to come. It shows the highest level of care the Ramirez name takes in their guitars. The Legado represents the pinnacle of the Ramirez line, a guitar built for the most demanding professional player or the serious collector seeking the ultimate expression of the classical guitar's potential. It's an investment in musical excellence and a piece of guitar-making history.

Takamine GC5-NAT

The Takamine GC5 looks great, plays smoothly, and delivers beautiful nylon-string sound. If you're on a budget, you'll be happy to know that the surprisingly affordable GC5 punches above its price range with a fan-braced solid spruce top and genuine black walnut back and sides for balanced, full-bodied tone. This instrument's comfortable mahogany neck and rosewood fingerboard feels just right. Other features include a dovetail neck joint, a lovely inlaid mosaic rosette, a rosewood headcap, gold tuners, and a gorgeous gloss finish.

Takamine GC6E-NAT

The Takamine GC-6CE is a nylon-string classical guitar that was designed with the player in mind. Every single design decision was carefully considered to deliver the most comprehensive playing experience possible. The body has a solid spruce top with walnut back and sides that create a well-balanced tone and bell-like ring. It has an oval-shaped soundhole that sculpts the airwaves as they leave the body, and for plugged-in performances, Takamine electronics reproduce the natural acoustic character of the instrument. The Takamine GC-6CE is the type of guitar that is easy to pick up and incredibly hard to put down.
